About Me

Hailing from the Bronx, Ryan is a CS graduate with a thriving career in Quality Assurance Engineering. His specialities include C, C++, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and React.js. Known for his sarcastic and dry humor, Ryan enjoys a more lighthearted approach to life, surrounding himself with those who love to laugh and smile.
